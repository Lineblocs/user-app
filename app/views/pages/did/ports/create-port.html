Port Number to lineblocs

when you try to port a number to lineblocs we try to work with your provider to port your number to our carrier and our app.
this process can sometimes can take upto 6 weeks.

you will be notified in email regarding the status of your port request as any change takes place or a date
is established for when the port request will be completed.

below are some things to note before trying to port a number:

- the number you are trying port should not have been disconnected by the provider
- no dispute should be open with the porting number
- porting number should not be scheduled for disconnection
- the porting number should not have any contract with the provider
- the porting number you port must be available in a rate center we support
to find a list of available rate centers please view Lineblocs Supported Rate Centers

if the number you are trying to port meets these requirements please also note that you will need to provide
the following documents:

letter of authorization (LOA) - a letter of authorization from the number owner
customer service record (CSR) - a customer service record
recent invoice from your provider - an invoice dated no longer than 90 days from the day you make the port
request.
